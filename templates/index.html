How to Play

How to Create a Lobby: Input your name and press Create New Room.

How to Join a Lobby: Input your name and room code, and then press Join Room

The game can be played with 2 to 32768 players. The game starts with the Room Host.

You have to write an English word that is no shorter than 3 letters.

If the word is too short, it doesn't get accepted.

If the word is a banned word, it doesn't get accepted, and the player loses a life.

If the player doesn't submit a valid word within 15 seconds, the player loses a life.

The last player standing wins!
